How it works

Compliance in three steps, not three months

Describe your policy once. Reso generates the proofs, keeps the data encrypted, and settles the outcome on-chain.

01

Simulate with ZK proofs

Run any transaction through the simulator. Reso produces a zero-knowledge proof that policy was met — the underlying data never leaves your machine.

02

Enforce encrypted policy

Spending limits and rules run over encrypted values with FHE. Approvals require a threshold of keyholders — no single party can peek.

03

Settle on Stellar

Verified outcomes settle through Soroban with claimable-balance escrow, SEP-8 approval, and instant revocation baked in.

Features

Everything you need to ship compliant privacy

Nine production-grade primitives, one engine. Composable, auditable, and open.

Proofs

ZK-Proof Transaction Simulator

Dry-run any transaction and generate a succinct proof that it satisfies policy before it ever touches the ledger.

FHE

FHE Spending Limits

Enforce budgets and caps directly over Paillier-encrypted amounts — the number stays secret end to end.

Identity

Threshold + Time-Locked Disclosure

Shamir 3-of-5 secret sharing means identity can only be unsealed by consensus, after a mandatory delay.

Revocation

Real-Time Credential Revocation

Merkle-tree accumulators let you revoke a credential and have verifiers reject it in the same block.

Scaling

Recursive Proof Folding

Nova-style IVC folds thousands of checks into one constant-size proof — verify a whole history in milliseconds.

Escrow

ZK-Gated Claimable Balance Escrow

Funds unlock only when a valid proof is presented — programmable escrow with privacy guarantees.

SEP-8

Regulated Asset Approval

Full SEP-8 approval flow so issuers can gate transfers of regulated assets with on-chain policy.

SEP-12

Anchor KYC Bridge

Bridge existing anchor KYC via SEP-12 without duplicating or exposing customer PII.

Enforcement

Clawback-Triggered Revocation

Tie Stellar clawbacks to automatic credential revocation for airtight enforcement.

Open core

Self-host or cloud

Run the whole engine yourself with Docker, or deploy in minutes. FastAPI backend, vanilla frontend, no lock-in.
