What is a Compliance Soulbound Token?

Unlike regular tokens, a CST cannot be transferred. It is permanently bound to the wallet that passed KYC. KYC attestations cannot be sold, borrowed, or faked — they live with the identity, not the money. This is the first implementation on Stellar.

How ZK Proof of Reserves Works

The system computes a reserve ratio (reserves ÷ liabilities) and stores only a ZK commitment on-chain. Anyone can verify solvency (ratio ≥ 1.0) from the commitment without ever learning the exact amounts. Prevents bank runs while proving trustworthiness — a first for Stellar stablecoin issuers.
