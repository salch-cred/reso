💡 What is a Compliance Soulbound Token?

Unlike regular tokens, a CST cannot be transferred. It is permanently bound to the wallet that passed KYC. This means KYC attestations cannot be sold, borrowed, or faked — they live with the identity, not the money. This is the first implementation on Stellar.

🔐 How ZK Proof of Reserves Works

The system computes a reserve ratio (reserves ÷ liabilities) and stores only a ZK commitment (cryptographic hash) on-chain. Anyone can verify solvency (ratio ≥ 1.0) from the commitment without ever learning the exact reserve or liability amounts. This prevents bank runs while proving trustworthiness — a world-first for Stellar stablecoin issuers.
