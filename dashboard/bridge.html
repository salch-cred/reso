How It Works
1

Authenticate

Sign in to your Reso account linked to a Stellar testnet wallet.

2

Generate Proof

The Reso oracle compiles your KYC tier, sanctions status, and spending limits into a signed ZK proof.

3

Select Chain

Choose a destination chain — Ethereum, Solana, Cosmos, or Polygon.

4

Bridge & Verify

The proof is relayed and verified on the destination chain. No re-KYC required.
